INI CET 2024 Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • Aspiring students should have passed their qualifying exams.
INI CET 2024 Syllabus

AIIMS New Delhi has not specified any syllabus for the exam. But it will normally consist of questions from the qualifying exams.

INI CET 2024 Pattern
  • The exam pattern for the exam has not yet been published.
  • It may consist of the previous pattern of AIIMS PG, JIPMER, PGIMER, and NIMHANS entrance exams. So, students should go through these patterns.
INI CET 2024 Dates
  • The exam is held in the months of May/June.

How to apply for INI CET 2024?

Aspiring candidates can apply online by visiting the official website of AIIMS Delhi i.e., https://www.aiims.edu/en.html, and follow the instructions given there.

INI CET 2024 Fees

There is no fee recommended from AIIMS Delhi as of now.

The exam will be conducted in various selected cities all over the country.
